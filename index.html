Hong Family Foundation

The Hong Family Foundation supports organizations and research initiatives that drive scientific progress, human well-being, and responsible exploration. We make strategic grants in the following priority areas:

Human Health & Education

  • Pediatric research — Advancing medical breakthroughs to improve the health and lives of children.
  • Science and engineering education — Strengthening STEM education to inspire the next generation of innovators and problem-solvers.

Sustainable Energy & Environment

  • Renewable energy research and advocacy — Supporting the development and adoption of clean energy technologies for a sustainable future.
  • Deep-sea life exploration and marine biology research — Expanding our understanding of ocean ecosystems, biodiversity, and the frontiers of life on Earth.

Exploration & Discovery

  • Human space exploration research and advocacy — Advancing humanity’s knowledge of the cosmos and our capacity to explore beyond Earth.

Future Technology & Safety

  • Development of safe artificial intelligence to benefit humanity — Promoting the responsible advancement of AI systems that are aligned with human values and contribute positively to society.
